jQuest is a platform for journalism schools. It lets journalism students in Europe learn datajournalism, connect with one another and investigate collaboratively.

You can use jQuest either as a stand-alone datajournalism module, as support for your current datajournalism class or as an introduction. jQuest comes with 40+ courses that you can share with your students.

Curious about jQuest? Want to connect with hundreds of fellow future journalists in 15+ countries? Ask us for an account or lobby your school to get you in!
